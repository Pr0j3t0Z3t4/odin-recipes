Filet mignon

You don't need to go to a steakhouse to make the best filet mignon of your life. This filet mignon with balsamic glaze will quickly become a go-to in your special occasion rotation. Balsamic Glaze for Steak Ingredients

Steak Ingredients unodered list:

Steaks: You'll need two filet mignon steaks for this restaurant-worthy recipe. Seasonings: This filet mignon is simply seasoned with salt and pepper. Balsamic vinegar: Of course, you'll need balsamic vinegar. It's the star of the show! Wine: Choose a dry red wine, such as cabernet sauvignon. How to Make Filet Mignon with Balsamic Glaze

Step-by-step for steak preparation:

Season the steaks: Sprinkle the filet mignon steaks with salt and pepper.

Sear the steaks: Sear the steaks in a very hot pan until they're browned on both sides.

Make the sauce: Reduce the heat to medium-low, then add the vinegar and wine. Cover and cook for about four minutes. Flip the steaks, baste with the sauce, and continue cooking for about four more minutes.

Serve the steaks: Place the steaks on two warmed plates. Spoon the sauce over the meat and serve immediately.
